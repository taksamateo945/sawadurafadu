We are excited by who Cheryl is working with on new album

 Posted on July 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  280 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

As we all know, Cheryl Fernandez-Versini is currently in LA working on a follow up album to her top-ten hit ‘Only Human’, which was released back in November 2014.

Since confirming her return to the recording studio earlier this year, the X Factor judge has been keeping her ‘soldiers’ up dated with exactly how the recording process is going.

And late last week the Geordie babe posted a selfie on Instagram with the caption: “Studio”, showing that she is currently working hard in America on new music.

And we here at Celeb Mix are very very excited by who exactly Cheryl is working with.

A photo of Chezza in the studio was posted by renowned songwriter Heidi Rojas with the caption: “I had a blast writing with these beautiful people the last couple days. Safe to say the beat got nastyyyyy. @blowdaspeakers” (sic).

Heidi has a huge back catalogue of hit singles that she has co-written including the massive hit single ‘Wings’ by Little Mix and also previously worked with Cheryl on her massive number 1 hit single ‘Crazy Stupid Love’.

The other people that the ‘I Don’t Care’ hitmaker is currently making music with, include the music producer Wayne Wilkins, who is the man responsible for Cheryl’s huge number 1 singles, ‘Fight For This Love’, ‘Promise This’ and ‘Crazy Stupid Love’.

Wilkins has also produced a number of album tracks for Cheryl over the years and has worked with some of the biggest names in pop.
