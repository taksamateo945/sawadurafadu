Kourtney Kardashian Flaunts Her Beach Body While Vacationing With Scott Disick And Their Kids

 Posted on April 22, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  554 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Kourtney Kardashian and Scott Disick have vacationed together as reports swirl that things are over between Sofia Richie and Scott. Fans quickly noticed that Sofia didn’t wish Scott a happy birthday and many were concerned that the break up would be hard for Scott as he recently entered and left rehab. Now, it looks like things are going well for Scott and many are wondering if there is going to be another reunion in the Kardashian/Jenner clan. Many fans are convinced that Khloe Kardashian and Tristan Thompson are a couple again and now with Scott and Kourtney vacationing together, many are wondering if the couple has reunited as a family as well. Scott and Kourtney share three children together but never married. The 41-year-old eldest Kardashian sibling has won praise for the way she co-parented her children and included Sofia in family activities so she wouldn’t feel left out.

The Kardashians and Jenners wished Scott Disick a public happy birthday on their social media pages, but Sofia shared no message to Scott who turned 37-years-old on May 26, 2020.

Kourtney shared photos and videos of her western-style vacation that included horseback riding in Utah and a stay at the Amangiri Resort and fans were surprised when Scott Disick shared a photo from the same location!

Though the two didn’t share any photos or videos where they were together in the shot, it does appear that he may have been the person holding the camera or used a GoPro when someone videotaped Kourtney and the kids on horseback!

Additionally, Kourtney is showing off her beach body as she posed in a tank top and panty set by Skims. The underwear set could double as a bikini and Kourtney has gone swimming in her Skims in the past.
