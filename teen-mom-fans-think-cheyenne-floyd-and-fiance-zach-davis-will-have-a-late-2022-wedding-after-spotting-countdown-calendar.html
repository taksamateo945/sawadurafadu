Teen Mom fans think Cheyenne Floyd and fiance Zach Davis will have a late 2022 wedding after spottin

 Posted on June 22, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  454 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

TEEN Mom fans think Cheyenne Floyd and fiancé Zach Davis will have a late 2022 wedding.

The speculation comes after fans noticed a countdown calendar in the couple’s latest YouTube vlog.

Earlier this week, Cheyenne, 28, and Zach, 30, posted a video in which they shared updates about the Teen Mom OG star’s current pregnancy.

Eagle-eyed fans noticed that a countdown calendar appeared behind the engaged couple, which read 542 days.

A number of MTV fans took to the comments section of the YouTube video to wonder if the couple hinted at their wedding date with countdown.

One person wrote: “Is ur wedding in 542 days or something…? Does anyone else see tht on the counter N can’t wait for Baby Ace… I’m So Excited…”

Another noted: “I see 542 days for the wedding I can’t wait for that.”

A third fan commented: “I just adore your family!!! And I see your wedding is set for November 2022. So exciting!!!”

The couple first announced the pregnancy in December and have been keeping fans up to date with constant updates on the journey.

With only a short amount of time left before the couple’s baby boy arrives, the two appeared tired and worn out in their latest vlog.

The Teen Mom star explained that they took a little break from filming because they were exhausted and just wanted to live in the moment for a bit.

Now they’re playing catch-up on weeks 33-36 of her pregnancy, though Cheyenne is now 38-weeks pregnant.

A few months after announcing their pregnancy in December, Cheyenne and Zach got engaged at their baby shower in March.

While throwing a teddy bear-themed party, the father-to-be popped the question to Cheyenne.

Zach bought two diamond rings; one for his fiancée and one for his step-daughter-to-be, Ryder.

The reality star shares Ryder, four, with ex Corey Wharton.
