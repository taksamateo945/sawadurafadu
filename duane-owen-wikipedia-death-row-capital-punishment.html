Duane Owen Wikipedia

Duane Owen was a long-serving inmate on Florida’s death row, known for committing gruesome murders.

He was responsible for the rape and stabbing of a 14-year-old babysitter, Karen Slattery, on March 24, 1984, while the children she cared for were asleep.

Alcoholic parents and an abusive father marked Owen’s troubled upbringing.

After spending his early years in a Michigan orphanage, he settled in South Florida in 1982.

Owen had a history of encounters with law enforcement, including arrests for burglary, attempted murder, and sexual battery between 1982 and 1984.

However, it was the 1984 murders of Karen Slattery and another victim, Georgianna Worden, that led to his long imprisonment in the Florida Department of Corrections.

Owen was executed on June 15, 2023, through lethal injection at Florida State Prison.

His defense team argued insanity, but his convictions and the severity of his crimes prevailed.
