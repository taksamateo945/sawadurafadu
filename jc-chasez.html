JC Chasez Biography

JC Chasez is best known as Singer, songwriter, dancer, record producer, actor who has an estimated Net Worth of $16 Million. Singer-songwriter and producer best known as a former member of the boy band NSYNC. He went on to produce music for such artists as Matthew Morrison and David Archuleta. He was also a judge on the MTV dance competition America's Best Dance Crew.  He was born on August 8,1976, Bowie, MD. In 2014, he toured with the show Jesus Christ Superstar in the role of Pontius Pilate.  He began his career in entertainment with The Mickey Mouse Club and went on to attend Bowie High School in Maryland.  He and Justin Timberlake helped turn NSYNC into one of the biggest acts of the 1990s.  He has two siblings, a younger brother named Tyler and a younger sister named Heather.  His zodiac sign is Leo.
