The Bachelorette Rachel Lindsay And Bryan Abasolo Are Married Heres Why The Bride Chose A Gor

 Posted on April 3, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  430 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Rachel Lindsay and Bryan Abasolo are married! Two years after she chose him in The Bachelorette finale, the couple has said: “I Do.”

People magazine was first to break the news Lindsay and Abasolo got hitched at the Royalton Suites Cancún in Mexico on Saturday. The bride wore wearing a Randi Rahm gown to walk down the aisle to the man of her dreams.

“Rachel was the epitome of elegance and grace as she walked down the aisle towards Bryan. The ceremony was filled with so much joy, love, and laughter. As they exchanged their personal handwritten vows, you could feel the unbridled emotion and powerful connection between them,” the couple’s wedding planner Michael Russo revealed to the weekly magazine.

Lindsay and Abasolo not only had close friends and family in attendance but a few Bachelor Nation pals were there too. Kristina Schulman, Astrid Loch, and Bibiana Julian made the trip to Mexica to watch their pals become husband and wife.

In the days leading up to their wedding, the newlyweds have teased their nuptials on social media. They were more than happy to share some of the behind the scenes moments with their followers.

The former The Bachelorette, kicked things off by asking her fiancé if he was ready for their big day.

“You ready B?” she captioned a loving photo of her and her man.

“READY! Sooooo, Meet me at the alter in your white dress… let’s get married!” the groom to be wrote in the comments section of Lindsay’s Instagram post.

On Friday, Abasolo shared a photo of him and his blushing bride with the caption “Off to a little adventure! #RnB.”

During wedding planning, Lindsay shared with People magazine why she chose Randi Rahm as her designer. It was because she had worn several of Randi’s dresses on The Bachelorette. The blushing bride also admitted what she was looking for in her one of a kind wedding dress.

“Brian’s always used to seeing me strong, in a bold outfit and in a pantsuit, because that’s my go-to. I thought, ‘I want to wear something that’s going to bring a tear to Brian’s eye.’ Most people see me a certain way, but Brian sees a softer side to me. I want to wear a dress that captures that,” Lindsay explained.

Two years ago, viewers watched as Bryan Abasolo popped the question to The Bachelorette Rachel Lindsay. It was one of the most romantic Bachelor Nation proposals to date. Now the happy couple is husband and wife!
