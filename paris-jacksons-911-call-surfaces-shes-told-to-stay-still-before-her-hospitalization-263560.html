Paris Jacksons 911 Call Surfaces: Shes Told To Stay Still Before Her Hospitalization

 Posted on February 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  280 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Paris Jackson was reportedly hospitalized on March 16, and the neighbor of her BF Gabriel Glenn was the one who made the call to 911.

It seems that The Blast obtained the audio and in the call that’s been released yesterday, the confused neighbor was telling an emergency dispatcher that Paris’ BF knocked on his door and told him to call 911.

The neighbor was not aware of the urgency of the situation, and the dispatcher instructed the caller to check if Paris was alert.

It’s presumed that Paris was alert as the dispatcher then said to ‘have her stay still’ and to ‘give her nothing to eat or drink,’ as reported by the online magazine Hollywood Life.

Before this, Paris had denied that something happened. Not too long ago, an insider report claimed that Michael’s daughter thought the rumors were very ‘upsetting’ and she really wants her close ones to know that she is alright and that she did not try to take her life.

An insider told Hollywood Life that ‘Paris feels hurt and upset the media won’t leave her alone when it comes to her mental health as does her family. Paris was receiving calls and texts when the TMZ report came out, and she told everyone she was OK and kept insisting not to worry and that she is fine.’

After the initial report which claimed that Paris tried to take her own life, it was reported that she actually cut her arm by mistake with scissors after a night of partying.
