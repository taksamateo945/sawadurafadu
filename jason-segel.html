Jason Segel is an actor, comedian, screenwriter, singer-songwriter, author, and producer from the United States. During his adolescence, he was fascinated by movies and even made his own. As a result, he decided to pursue a career as an actor. Similarly, he later collaborated with a well-known producer, Judd Apatow, on the high school drama-comedy series ‘Freaks and Geeks,’ and became a well-known face in the industry. Furthermore, he is well-known for his role as Marshall Eriksen in the CBS sitcom ‘How I Met Your Mother.’ This talented actor is also a screenwriter, author, musician, and songwriter, and his songs have been featured on several of his acting projects.

Jason Segel Age And Early Life

Jason Jordan Segel was born in Los Angeles, California on January 18, 1980. His father Alvin Segel is a lawyer, and his mother Jillian is a housewife. His two siblings, Adam Segel, his older half-brother, and Alison Segel, his younger sister, helped him grow up. He is 43 years old and his Zodiac sign is Capricorn.

Jason Segel Height & Weight

He is 6 feet 4 inches (193cm/1.93m) tall and weighs around 92 kg (203lbs). He has an average body shape, dark brown eyes, and brown hair.

Jason Segel Professional Life

In terms of his films, Jason made his debut in 1998 with ‘Can Hardly Wait.’ He also appeared in ‘Dead Man on Campus’ and ‘SLC Punk’ the same year, as Kyle and Mike, respectively. He then appeared in 2002’s ‘Slackers,’ 2003’s ’11:14,’ 2004’s ‘Lolli Love,’ and 2005’s ‘The Good Humor Man.’ Similarly, he had roles in ‘Bye Bye Benjamin’ and ‘Tenacious D in The Pick of Destiny,’ both of which had their scenes cut in 2006. Jason also appeared in the 2007 film ‘Knocked Up’ under his own name. In 2008, he also wrote and acted as Peter in the film ‘Forgetting Sarah Marshall.’ His other films include ‘I Love You, Man,’ released in 2009, ‘Get Him to the Greek,’ ‘Despicable Me,’ and ‘Gulliver’s Travels,’ released in 2010. In 2011, Segel landed uncredited roles in ‘Friends with Benefits,’ ‘Bad Teacher,’ and ‘Jeff, Who Lives at Home.’

He had a voice role in ‘Family Guy’ in 2009 and hosted an episode of ‘Saturday Night Live’ in 2011. Following that, he appeared in the 2020 series ‘Dispatches from Elsewhere,’ playing the role of Peter. He is also the series’ creator, writer, director, and executive producer. His most recent series was ‘Home Movie: The Princess Bride,’ which starred Mckenna Grace, Adam Sandler, Kaitlyn Dever, Elijah Wood, Zoe Deutch, Natalie Morales, and others.

Jason Segel Girlfriend & Dating

Jason Segel is currently unmarried, despite dating a number of celebrities. From 2001 to 2007, he was in a relationship with Linda Cardellini. Since 2014, this actor has been dating Alexis Mixter. In addition to his romantic life, he is an ordained minister with the Universal Life Church.
